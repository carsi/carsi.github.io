Design Strategy & Process

I believe design starts with people. As institutions have become more aware of user-centered design, they too have become a driving force to deliver quality experiences for their users. I use design thinking methods and storytelling to make a compelling case for users. Working closely with project managers and content experts, I am able to leverage their knowledge to co-create interactive experiences that serve the everyday person.

Typical steps in my process:

  1. Initial research on the subject matter and users
  2. Design strategy to align with the vision of the organization
  3. User experience design to determine user needs and motivations
  4. Ideation, including design methods and sketching wireframes
  5. High fidelity user interface design
  6. Testing through prototyping
